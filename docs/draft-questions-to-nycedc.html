To: GroceriesBrandDesign@edc.nyc
Subject: N.Y.C. Groceries Branding & Design Consultant RFP — clarification questions

Hello,

We are preparing a response to the N.Y.C. Groceries Branding & Design Consultant RFP and request clarification on the items below.

  1. Appendix 3 / Appendix 4 form versions. The RFP includes the M/WBE Narrative (Appendix 3) and the M/WBE Subconsultant Participation Plan (Appendix 4) as embedded templates. The versions currently posted on NYCEDC's Vendor Resources page are dated 03-13-2025. If the embedded and posted versions differ, should respondents complete the versions embedded in this RFP or the latest versions on the Vendor Resources page?

  2. Intent to Perform — required at proposal stage? Section III.C (Required Appendices) lists the M/WBE Narrative, the Subconsultant Participation Plan, the Doing Business Data Form, the Statement of Agreement, and the Appendix 6 cost proposal — but does not expressly list the Intent to Perform as Subcontractor form. The Participation Plan workbook, however, references an Intent to Perform form "for each firm listed." Please confirm whether a signed Intent to Perform form must be included with the proposal, and if so, whether it is required for all listed subconsultants (including non-M/WBE firms) or only for firms counted toward the 30% M/WBE goal.

  3. First-Tier Subcontractor Self-Perform Statement. NYCEDC's Vendor Resources page also includes a First-Tier Subcontractor Self-Perform Statement. Please confirm whether that form is required at proposal stage for this solicitation, or whether the required M/WBE proposal-stage documents are limited to the Appendix 3 Narrative and Appendix 4 Participation Plan.

  4. Consortium / team structure at proposal stage. For a proposal submitted by a lead Respondent with named subconsultants (rather than a formal joint venture), should all named subconsultants be listed in the Appendix 4 Participation Plan at proposal stage with estimated award amounts, even though the Maximum Contract Price is to be negotiated after selection?

  5. M/WBE 30% goal — measurement base and prime eligibility. Please clarify two points on the 30% M/WBE participation goal: (a) is the 30% measured against the total Maximum Contract Price, or against some other base; and (b) if the lead Respondent is itself a certified M/WBE, may the lead's own work toward the Services count toward the 30% goal, or must the 30% be allocated to M/WBE-certified subconsultants, suppliers, or contractors other than the lead?

  6. Doing Business Data Form — who submits. For a proposal that includes a lead Respondent and multiple named partner firms, should the Doing Business Data Form (Appendix 1) be submitted only by the lead Respondent at proposal stage, or by each named team entity?

  7. Page-limit scope. Section III caps the proposal at thirty (30) pages, "excluding the required appendices." Please confirm whether team member bios/resumes, the organizational overview, and visual examples of prior projects count toward the 30-page limit, or are treated as excluded appendices.

  8. Appendix 6 — Task 3 pricing assumptions. Task 3 (Architecture & Engineering Collaboration, anticipated 2027–2029) involves a brand deployment review for each store plus as-needed final submittal reviews before fabrication. May respondents price Task 3 based on clearly stated assumptions (e.g., the number of deployment and submittal reviews per store), or does NYCEDC have a preferred baseline assumption per store?
