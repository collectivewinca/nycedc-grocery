We are preparing a response to the N.Y.C. Groceries Branding & Design Consultant RFP and request clarification on the items below.

  1. Appendix 3 / Appendix 4 forms: The RFP states that the M/WBE Narrative Form and M/WBE Subconsultant Participation Plan are separate attachments. Can respondents use the current versions posted on NYCEDC's Vendor Resources page for this solicitation, or are there solicitation-specific versions that must be used?

  5. Appendix 6 pricing assumptions: For Task 3 (Architecture and Engineering Collaboration, anticipated 2027-2029), may respondents price based on clearly stated assumptions regarding the anticipated number of deployment reviews / submittal reviews, or does NYCEDC have a preferred baseline assumption per store?

Please confirm whether that form is required at proposal stage for this solicitation, or whether the required proposal-stage M/WBE documents are limited to the Appendix 3 narrative form, Appendix 4 participation plan, and the Intent to Perform form(s).
