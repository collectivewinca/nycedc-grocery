This site publishes the partner packet created from the NYCEDC N.Y.C. Groceries Branding & Design Consultant RFP, including official forms, outreach templates, intake tools, and draft clarification questions.

What is here

  • Converted HTML versions of the partner packet markdown files
  • Official NYCEDC forms copied into downloadable links
  • The original RFP PDF and a working participation-plan CSV

Critical verified points

  • Questions due June 12, 2026 at 5:00 PM ET
  • Proposal due June 30, 2026 at 4:00 PM ET
  • M/WBE goal is 30%
  • The participation plan form states that an Intent to Perform form is included for each listed firm
