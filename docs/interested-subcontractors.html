What this is — and isn't. This is not a list of competing primes (NYCEDC doesn't publish
who's bidding). It's the opposite: 21 firms that have signaled they want to subcontract on
N.Y.C. Groceries — a warm, pre-sorted partner pool, most already NYC M/WBE-certified. For DoChakki
(prime) these are recruitment leads, and they directly serve the 30% M/WBE goal.

Why this is a strategic gift

  • These firms already want in on this RFP — far warmer than cold outreach.

  • We can field a complete, all-NYC-M/WBE team covering every task — and document genuine
    good-faith M/WBE outreach (helps the 15% score) by sourcing from NYCEDC's own list.
