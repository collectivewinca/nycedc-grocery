[Partner Letterhead]

Date: [DATE]

Re: N.Y.C. Groceries Branding & Design Consultant RFP

This Letter of Intent confirms that [PARTNER LEGAL NAME] (“Partner”) intends to participate on the proposal being submitted by [LEAD RESPONDENT LEGAL NAME] (“Lead”) in response to the New York City Economic Development Corporation (“NYCEDC”) N.Y.C. Groceries Branding & Design Consultant RFP.

1. Intended role

If included in the proposal and if Lead is selected for award, Partner intends to serve as:

  • Role: [RESEARCH / ENVIRONMENTAL BRANDING / WAYFINDING / COPY / OTHER]
  • Primary scope: [SHORT DESCRIPTION]
  • Related RFP task(s): [TASK 1A / 1C / 2B / 3A / 4B / ETC.]

2. Commercial understanding

The parties currently anticipate that Partner's subcontract scope would be valued at approximately $[AMOUNT], representing approximately [X]% of the total proposed contract value, subject to final proposal pricing, negotiations, and award.

3. Certifications and proposal support

Partner confirms the following:

  1. Its legal name, address, and authorized signatory are accurately stated below.
  2. It will provide proposal support materials reasonably requested by Lead, including bios, project examples, references, NAICS code(s), and pricing inputs.
  3. If Partner is being counted toward M/WBE participation, it will provide its current certification information and supporting documentation.
  4. If Lead lists Partner in the NYCEDC participation plan, Partner will promptly review and sign the official Intent to Perform as Subcontractor form.
  5. Partner understands that final participation is contingent on Lead's submission strategy, NYCEDC's award, and a mutually executed subcontract.

4. Non-binding business terms

Except for Section 5 below, this Letter of Intent is a statement of present intent only and does not create a binding obligation for either party to enter into a subcontract. Any final subcontract will be subject to mutually agreed terms and any applicable NYCEDC requirements.

5. Limited binding terms

The parties agree that from the date of signature through the proposal submission date:

  1. Partner will reserve reasonable availability to support proposal preparation.
  2. Partner will not knowingly provide conflicting scope/pricing information to Lead for this same proposal.
  3. Any confidential information exchanged for proposal preparation will be used only for this pursuit.
