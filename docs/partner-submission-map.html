Use the official NYCEDC forms in ../official-nycedc-forms/ for the final submission. These templates are for recruiting, collecting inputs, and speeding signatures.

Verified RFP points

  • Questions due: Friday, June 12, 2026 at 5:00 PM ET
  • Questions email: GroceriesBrandDesign@edc.nyc
  • Proposal due: Tuesday, June 30, 2026 at 4:00 PM ET
  • Proposal format: single zip upload; main proposal in searchable PDF; required appendices included
  • M/WBE goal: 30%
  • Do not submit brand concepts in the proposal

What must be signed and/or submitted

1. Official Intent to Perform as Subcontractor form

  • File: ../official-nycedc-forms/Intent-to-Perform-as-Subcontractor.pdf
  • Who signs: prime + each listed subcontractor/subconsultant
  • Why it matters: the current NYCEDC participation plan states that an Intent to Perform form must be included for each firm listed on the participation plan
  • Minimum fields needed:
  • prime legal name
  • subcontractor legal name
  • addresses and phone numbers
  • subcontractor federal ID number
  • NYCEDC contract number
  • projected start date / completion date
  • work to be performed
  • price of work to be performed
  • title/date/signature for both parties

2. MWBE/DBE Participation Plan workbook

  • File: ../official-nycedc-forms/MWBE-DBE-Participation-Plan-03-13-2025.xlsx
  • Who provides inputs: every expected team member
  • Important: this plan must list all consultants and contractors expected to work on the project, whether MWBE/DBE or not
  • Key fields per firm:
  • contractor / consultant name
  • prime or sub
  • MWBE/DBE status
  • ethnicity group
  • award amount
  • description of services
  • NAICS codes
  • contracting party
  • contracting party MWBE/DBE status
  • MWBE/DBE amount

3. MWBE Narrative Form

  • File: ../official-nycedc-forms/MWBE-Narrative-Form-03-13-2025.docx
  • Who signs: respondent / authorized person
  • Partner inputs needed:
  • role on team
  • whether the firm is certified MWBE
  • strategy for participation
  • past similar projects
  • references
  • in-house M/WBE contact

4. Doing Business Data Form

  • File: ../official-nycedc-forms/Doing-Business-Data-Form.pdf
  • RFP says: respondent must submit this form
  • Open question: whether consortium members / non-lead entities also submit at proposal stage

5. First-Tier Subcontractor Self-Perform Statement

  • NYCEDC's vendor resources page includes this form as a general M/W/DBE resource
  • It is not expressly cited in this RFP packet
  • Treat it as not yet verified for proposal-stage submission unless NYCEDC confirms it is required for this solicitation

What to collect from every proposed partner now

  1. Legal entity name, address, phone, main contact, signatory name/title
  2. Federal tax ID / EIN
  3. Exact scope tied to RFP tasks
  4. Proposed fee or estimated subcontract value
  5. NAICS code(s)
  6. MWBE certification details, if applicable
  7. Certificate PDF and expiration date, if applicable
  8. 2-3 relevant project examples
  9. Bio / resume for key person
  10. Confirmation they will sign the official Intent to Perform form

Practical rule

Do not list a partner in the participation plan unless they are ready to be priced, named, and sign.
