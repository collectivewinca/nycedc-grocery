## ⚠️ One certification nuance to confirm

NYCEDC's M/WBE program credit generally keys off **NYC SBS certification**. **NY State** MWBE
certification and **national/out-of-state** WBE certs (e.g., RSM's Texas/NWBOC) do **not**
automatically count for a NYC contract. Two Twelve and JPD both appear to carry NYC certification,
but confirm each firm's *NYC SBS* status (not just NYS) before counting them toward the 30%.

## How to verify (do this before naming any firm in Appendix 4)

1. **NYC SBS Online Directory of Certified Businesses** — search the firm's legal name; confirm
   certification type (MBE/WBE/M/WBE), NAICS codes, and expiration date. Save the PDF record.
2. **NY State MWBE directory** (ny.newnycontracts.com) — secondary confirmation for state credit.
3. Capture each firm's **federal EIN, NAICS codes, certification number, and expiration** into
   `06_participation-plan-working-sheet.csv` — those are the exact fields Appendix 4 needs.
