# M/WBE participation plan — how to win the 15%

The M/WBE plan is 15% of the score and a compliance gate (a non-conforming plan can sink the bid).
This is how to fill **Appendix 3 (Narrative)** and **Appendix 4 (Participation Plan)** so the 30%
goal is met credibly and ties to the pricing worksheet. Goal: **30% of contract value to certified
M/WBEs.**

> **Verify FIRST:** confirm each partner's **NYC SBS** certification (the certification NYCEDC
> credits) in the Online Directory of Certified Businesses — not just NY State or national WBE.
> Capture certification number, category, ethnicity/gender class, NAICS, and expiration. See the
> verification steps in `07_partner-firm-shortlist.md`. *Unverified = do not count it.*

## Strategy: 30% to core scope, not token roles

Our M/WBE dollars flow to **Two Twelve** (MBE+WBE) doing *real* environmental/wayfinding work
(Tasks 2B & 3) — work the brand genuinely needs. That's the strongest possible posture: integral,
not bolted on. If the numbers fall short of 30%, add **JPD Studio** (M/WBE) on the identity side
before reducing anyone's scope.

> Pending clarification **Q5**: confirm whether the 30% base is the total Maximum Contract Price and
> whether a certified prime could self-count. Adjust the plan once NYCEDC answers (posted June 18).
